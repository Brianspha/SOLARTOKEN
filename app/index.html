How It Works



For Everyone:


Open an Account

Opening an account with us gives you access to community member features such as access to the Token Trade

Buy Tokens

Tokens represent 1 kWh of energy production from the Power Purchase Agreements

Use Renewable Energy

Use the bought Energy Tokens for everyday power needs



For Investors:


Open an Account

Opening an account with us gives you access to community member features such as access to the Token Trade

Buy Tokens

Tokens represent 1 kWh of energy production from the Power Purchase Agreements

Generate Income

Income from Usage or Power Purchase Agreements with reward paid to token through smart contract

Exchange Tokens

Store, trade or liquidate tokens from the Platform Dashboard. Swop Project tokens for Platform tokens (XSS). Trade XSS on Approved Crypto Exchanges



For Renewable Energy Project Developers:


Apply for Listing

Submit an application for a Project to be listed on the Platform. Our EPC Team will evaluate the Project

Tokenization and Listing

On acceptance project generation capacity in kWh will be tokenised. The project is then listed on Platform

Fund Raising

Project tokens are purchased by Community investors via the Platform Dashboard

Project Commencement

Once more than 80% of the funds have been raised, the Project can commence. Funds are now released on an approved Budget Plan



Switch to Renewable Energy

Swop all or part of your current Electricity Bill for the usage of Solar Equipment and own the equipment within 10 years. Making savings in the process and switch a Living Expense into an Investment

Utilising the Power of DLT

SolarSwop is using Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT) to launch a Renewable Energy Fund via an ICO / ITO (Initial Token Offering) to fund the development of DLT Energy Trading Platform, a market place for energy providers, investors, end user finance, and project developer finance
